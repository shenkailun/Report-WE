NHS DepthVisor Surgery Camera System

Team 20 - Kyla Aguillo, Venet Kukran, Kailun Shen

Our team's surgery camera system is a web app for surgeons wishing to share their surgery experience with medical students for training purposes.

Record

Use Kinect camera attached to surgical glasses that is connected to the web app to record surgeries with timestamps capabilities (by voice) to highlight key events

Store

Recorded videos will be stored in 3D along with timestamps and video descriptions

Share

Access to videos can be given to students
